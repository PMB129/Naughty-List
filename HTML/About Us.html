Naughty Dog

The Game Creator

A flagship first-party studio inside PlayStation Studios was established in 1984 and is one of the greatest video game development company in the industry. Naughty Dog is responsible for some of the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful games on Sony's PlayStation platforms, from the original Crash Bandicoot and Jak and Daxter series to modern franchises like Uncharted and The Last of Us.

Naughty Dog has garnered hundreds of industry and media awards and also gained a passionate fan base of millions of players around the globe for their use of cutting-edge technology and evocative, character-driven storytelling developments in their creations. The awards that the game development company has received are "Studio of the Year" award at the 2013 VGX, 2013 Golden Joystick Awards, and 2020 Golden Joystick Awards.
