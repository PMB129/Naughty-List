Uncharted 4

A THIEF'S END

Sam and Drake set out to uncover Libertalia, the pirate utopia deep in the jungles of Madagascar, in search of Captain Henry Avery's long-lost riches. UNCHARTED 4: A Thief's End takes players on a voyage around the world in quest of Avery's treasure, via jungle isles, far-flung towns, and snow-capped summits.

Summary

Game Plot

Nathan Drake has retired from global travel three years after his last expedition, and was living a peaceful life with his wife but he still feels the overwhelming lure of adventure. When Nathan's brother Sam Drake reappears, promising an incredible adventure and hidden riches, it doesn't take much to persuade Nathan to pack his belongings and travel to Libertalia. As he reunites with his long-lost brother, Samuel, and attempts to save him from a ruthless tyrant by embarking on a new adventure involving the discovery of Captain Henry Avery's fabled pirate treasure.

Characters

Nathan Drake

Nathan "Nate" Drake (born Nathan Morgan) is the main character in the Uncharted video game series. He is also the primary protagonist and controllable character in the video game franchise. He is married to Elena Fisher in this particular series.

Elena Fisher

Elena Fisher is a former American journalist, TV celebrity, and international correspondent who is one of the Uncharted series' two deuteragonists.She is married to Nathan Drake, with whom she has a daughter called Cassie, as of A Thief's End.

Samuel Drake

Samuel "Sam" Drake (born Samuel Morgan) is a deuteragonist, a minor playable character, Nathan Drake's older brother and a professional thief and treasure seeker. He returns to seek Nate's assistance in locating Henry Avery's lost wealth.

Victor Sullivan

Victor "Sully" Sullivan is one of the Uncharted series' two deuteragonists. Sully appears as a prominent character and ally to Drake and his comrades throughout the game, frequently accompanying them on their numerous adventures.

Rafe Adler

Rafe Adler is an American treasure hunter and businessman who appears in Uncharted 4. He is, a previous colleague of brothers Nathan and Sam Drake, joined forces with them to find Henry Avery's buried fortune, but subsequently competed with them.

Nadine Ross

In the Uncharted series, Nadine Ross is a recurrent character. She is the former head of the paramilitary group Shoreline, which was eventually recruited by industrialist Rafe Adler to assist him in his search for the legendary pirate colony of Libertalia.
