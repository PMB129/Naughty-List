The Last Of Us II

Living amongst a thriving community of survivors has allowed them peace and stability, despite the constant threat of the infected and other, more desperate survivors. When a violent event disrupts that peace, Ellie embarks on a relentless journey to carry out justice and find closure.

Summary

Game Plot

Ellie and Joel have settled in Jackson, Wyoming, five years after their journey in the post-apocalyptic United States. Despite the continual threat of the infected and other, more desperate survivors, living amid a thriving community of survivors has provided them with tranquility and stability. When a horrific occurrence interrupts the peace, Ellie sets out on a never-ending quest for justice and closure. The game focuses on two playable characters in a post-apocalyptic United States whose lives intertwine: Ellie, who sets out for revenge after suffering a tragedy, and Abby, a soldier who becomes involved in a conflict between her militia and a religious cult.

Characters

Ellie

Ellie Williams is the central character of The Last of Us series. When introduced as a fourteen-year-old survivor, Ellie is "mature beyond her years" - a result of the circumstances of her environment.

Joel Miller

After losing his only daughter, Sarah in the early stages of the outbreak, Joel became a ruthless and cynical smuggler eventually tasked with smuggling and protecting Ellie Williams, a young girl who was the key to mankind's survival. Joel eventually formed a strong bond with her.

Abby

A member of the Fireflies broken by tragedy when Joel Miller killed her father, Abby made it her mission to avenge Jerry's demise. However, it is only after her vengeance that she embarks on a journey with companion Lev and rediscover's purpose in her life.

Dina

Dina is the deuteragonist of Ellie's story in The Last of Us Part II. She is Ellie's partner and assists her on her quest for revenge.

Jesse

Jesse is a supporting character in The Last of Us Part II. He was Ellie's friend and a leader of the patrol groups in Jackson. Jesse is one of the characters who join Ellie on her quest for revenge.

Tommy Miller

Tommy survived the early years of the outbreak with Joel. However, the two separated as Tommy sought a more meaningful life than mere survival, leading him to join the Fireflies and later seek redemption through his wife, Maria, and the community of Jackson.
